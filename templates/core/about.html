Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Food

Catrin Boys started as a small family catering business in Kozhikode, Kerala — built on the belief that great food brings people together. Over the years, we've grown into one of the most trusted catering teams in the region.

From intimate family dinners to grand weddings with 1000+ guests, we bring the same care, freshness, and love to every single plate. Our chefs are trained in traditional Kerala cuisine as well as modern continental cooking, giving you the best of both worlds.
